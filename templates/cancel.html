Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2025

We understand that plans change. This policy outlines the cancellation and refund terms that apply to lodge bookings and safari packages made through Safarii.

Lodge Bookings

30+ days before check-in

Full Refund

Cancellations made 30 or more days before your check-in date are eligible for a full refund, minus any processing fees.

15 – 29 days before check-in

50% Refund

Cancellations made between 15 and 29 days before your check-in date are eligible for a 50% refund of the total booking amount.

Under 14 days before check-in

No Refund

Cancellations made within 14 days of your check-in date are non-refundable. We recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances.

Safari Packages

60+ days before departure

Full Refund

Cancellations made 60 or more days before your departure date are eligible for a full refund, minus the booking deposit.

30 – 59 days before departure

50% Refund

Cancellations made between 30 and 59 days before departure are eligible for a 50% refund of the total package cost.

Under 30 days before departure

No Refund

Cancellations made within 30 days of your departure date are non-refundable. We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance for all safari packages.

How to Cancel

To request a cancellation, please contact us through our contact page with your booking reference number and the reason for cancellation.

Refunds, where applicable, will be processed within 7 – 14 business days to the original payment method.

Exceptions

In the event of extraordinary circumstances including natural disasters, government-issued travel advisories, or park closures Safarii will work with guests on a case-by-case basis to offer date changes, credits, or refunds where possible.

Safarii reserves the right to cancel a booking in cases of force majeure, unavailability, or operator issues. In such cases, a full refund will be issued.
